ABOUT iMealPrep

Picture yourself in this situation:

You, like many others, don't feel like making constant trips to the grocery store but you don't know what to make.

Open the fridge? Freezer? Pantry??

What about browsing social media for something tasty?

If you have struggled with any of these, then iMealPrep is the perfect companion for helping you plan your meals each week.

As somebody who meal preps to follow a fitness journey to build muscle, meal prepping was sometimes challenging for a number of reasons. From shortness of time to complexity of the recipe, I can relate. Whether the reason was because of the recipe's complexity, or simply lack of time, I sought to make a change to how I approached meal prepping.

If I could tell my phone what I had available in my fridge, freezer, and pantry, what if it gave me suggestions on what to make? Thus became the genesis of iMealPrep.

Not only does iMealPrep help make meal prepping easier for the average Joe, but it can also help anybody who is on a fitness journey, whether they are building muscle, losing weight, bulking, or cutting. With complete measurements of macro-nutrients in each recipe, you can hit your fitness goals with ease.

If you are ready to bring out your best cookware for your next delicious meals, count on iMealPrep.
